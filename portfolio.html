Creative Process

Below you can find an outline of my creative process which helps me to deliver user-centered, goal-driven designs.

  1. Identify & Clarify

    One of the most essential parts in the process if identifying and defining all aspects of the project goals, audience market, competitors, specifications and requirements.

  2. Discover & Strategize

    My goal with each project is to deliver solid and real-world solutions that achieve actual business needs, we do this through idea generation and exploration which parts make sense.

  3. Designing An Experience

    The creative part of the process where we take everything we learned and use it to direct a solution-based design approach that aims to solve the issues and achieve our set goals
